Why Should You Care?

Air pollution is a big problem that must be addressed immediately.

Air pollution has many health risks. The pollutants in the air can irritate our lungs and eyes. They may cause different types of respiratory diseases. The effects are even worse for people with asthma.
About 93 percent of children breathe polluted air. This is alarming because they are still developing. This may affect their development and cause chronic diseases later in life.
It is a human right. 155 states are legally obligated to provide their citizens with a healthy environment. This includes clean air.
