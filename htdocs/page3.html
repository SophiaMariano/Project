What Can We Do To Solve Air Pollution?

Here are three ways you can help in solving air pollution.

Reduce the Use of Vehicles

A lot of vehicles produce tons of greenhouse gases that pollute the environment. In order to reduce the pollution caused by this, you could avoid the use of vehicles. If possible, walk or ride a bicycle. These are more environmental friendly ways to travel. Furthermore, if you need to go somewhere a bit far, you could take public transportation. By doing so, there would be less vehicles contributing to air pollution.

Practice Proper Waste Management

Another cause of air pollution is improper waste management. Streets, rivers, and landfills are occupied by hills of garbage due to failure to manage waste properly. By practicing proper segregation and by reducing, reusing, and recycling your waste, this can be avoided.

Conserve Electricity

When you consume electricity, fossil fuels are being emitted and this contributes to air pollution. Another thing you can do it reduce your electrical consumption. Turn off the lights and other appliances if not being used.
